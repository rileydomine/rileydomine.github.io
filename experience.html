Experience

Many examples of my leadership in Boys4Bikes include managing social media platforms such as the Instagram account and website, and overseeing the funds and finances of each operation. Last year in particular, I guided the entire group on the logistics on what we could do better to make more profits as well as introduced many possible sponsors which turned out to be a success.
